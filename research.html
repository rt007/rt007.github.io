Research Focus

My research focuses on emerging technologies, particularly digital twin technology and its applications in smart cities, risk and emergency management, and artificial intelligence. I lead multiple projects that develop and implement digital twin solutions and explore their practical applications across various industries. The research is carried out in collaboration with stakeholders, including Dublin Fire Brigade, Bentley Systems, and Dublin City Council, with the aim of translating emerging technologies into real-world operational and societal benefits.
